# Property Tax Rates - Sources and Verification

This document lists all property tax rates used in the closing cost calculator and their sources for verification.

## Important Note
Property tax rates are typically expressed as a rate per $100 of assessed value. To convert to a percentage:
- $1.09 per $100 = 1.09% effective rate
- Rates may vary slightly based on assessment ratios and local tax districts

## General Sources for Verification

1. **Tax Foundation:** https://taxfoundation.org/data/all/state/property-taxes-by-state-county/
   - Comprehensive county-level data
   - Updated annually

2. **Chase Property Tax by State:** https://www.chase.com/personal/mortgage/education/finding-a-home/property-tax-by-state
   - State-level averages
   - Good for verification

3. **SmartAsset Property Tax Calculator:** https://smartasset.com/taxes/property-tax-calculator
   - County-specific calculators
   - Can verify individual counties

4. **Official County Websites:**
   - Each county's official website (links provided above)
   - Most accurate source for current rates
   - Usually found under "Finance" or "Tax Assessor" departments

## Disclaimer for Users

Property tax rates are estimates based on general county rates. Actual property taxes may vary based on:
- Specific property assessments
- Local tax districts
- Special assessments
- Property exemptions
- Assessment ratios

Users should verify rates with their local tax assessor's office for the most accurate information.
